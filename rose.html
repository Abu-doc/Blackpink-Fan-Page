ROSÉ

Roseanne Park, known mononymously as ROSÉ, is a New Zealand-born South Korean singer, dancer, and the main vocalist of BLACKPINK. ROSÉ's mesmerizing voice and emotional delivery have earned her a dedicated fanbase. Her solo career has also flourished, further solidifying her status as one of the most influential figures in K-pop.

Personal Information

  • Full Name: Roseanne Park
  • Date of Birth: February 11, 1997
  • Nationality: South Korean (Born in New Zealand)
  • Position in BLACKPINK: Main Vocalist, Lead Dancer
  • Debut: August 8, 2016 (with BLACKPINK)
  • Known For: Her soulful voice and captivating performances

Achievements

  • Successful Solo Debut with "On The Ground" – A Billboard Charting Hit
  • Featured in "Vogue" and Other Prestigious Fashion Magazines
  • Global Ambassador for Yves Saint Laurent
  • Multiple Award-Winning Artist – Including MTV Video Music Awards
