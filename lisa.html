LISA

Lalisa Manoban, known mononymously as LISA, is a Thai rapper, singer, dancer, and the youngest member of the iconic K-pop group BLACKPINK. With her unparalleled talent, she has gained global recognition as one of the best performers in the music industry. LISA's style is a mix of fierce rap delivery, powerful dance moves, and a distinct fashion sense that inspires millions worldwide.

Personal Information

  • Full Name: Lalisa Manoban
  • Date of Birth: March 27, 1997
  • Nationality: Thai
  • Position in BLACKPINK: Lead Rapper, Dancer, Maknae
  • Debut: August 8, 2016 (with BLACKPINK)
  • Known For: Her unique rap style, powerful dance moves, and flawless stage presence

Achievements

  • First Female K-pop Idol to Reach 50 Million Followers on Instagram
  • Debuted as a Solo Artist with "LALISA" – a Record-Breaking Single
  • Featured in Forbes' 30 Under 30 Asia List
  • Multiple Award-Winning Artist – Including MTV Europe Music Awards, Teen Choice Awards, and more
  • Global Ambassador for Celine and HERA Beauty
