JENNIE

Kim Jennie, known mononymously as JENNIE, is a South Korean rapper, singer, and a member of BLACKPINK. Known for her fierce charisma and versatile vocals, JENNIE quickly became a fan favorite, showcasing her talent both as a solo artist and as part of the iconic group. Her style blends high fashion with edgy street style, and she's renowned for her influence in the K-pop industry.

Personal Information

  • Full Name: Kim Jennie
  • Date of Birth: January 16, 1996
  • Nationality: South Korean
  • Position in BLACKPINK: Main Rapper, Lead Vocalist
  • Debut: August 8, 2016 (with BLACKPINK)
  • Known For: Her powerful rap delivery and vocal versatility

Achievements

  • First Solo Single "SOLO" – A Global Hit
  • Featured in the "TIME" Magazine's 100 Next List
  • Multiple Award-Winning Artist – Including Mnet Asian Music Awards, and more
  • Global Ambassador for Calvin Klein, HERA, and more
