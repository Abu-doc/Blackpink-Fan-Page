JISOO

Kim Jisoo, known mononymously as JISOO, is a South Korean singer, dancer, and visual of BLACKPINK. She is known for her unique, powerful voice, and her ability to effortlessly blend elegance with charisma. As the group's visual, she also plays a crucial role in influencing K-pop's beauty standards and inspiring her millions of fans worldwide.

Personal Information

  • Full Name: Kim Jisoo
  • Date of Birth: January 3, 1995
  • Nationality: South Korean
  • Position in BLACKPINK: Lead Vocalist, Visual
  • Debut: August 8, 2016 (with BLACKPINK)
  • Known For: Her stunning visuals and captivating voice

Achievements

  • Successful Solo Debut with "ME" – A Sensational Track
  • Featured in Various Fashion Magazines, Including Vogue
  • Known for Charitable Work – Actively engaged in philanthropy
  • Global Ambassador for Dior Beauty
